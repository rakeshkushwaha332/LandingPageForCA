About us

Bansal & Agrawal

Established in 2002, Bansal & Agrawal, Chartered Accountants brings over two and half decades of trusted expertise across a broad spectrum of financial and regulatory domains. The firm has cultivated deep proficiency in Central and State Government Subsidy Schemes, Income Tax, Audit, Accounting, Finance, and a wide range of Regulatory Compliance Services.


Our extensive experience, coupled with a client-focused approach, enables us to deliver strategic, timely, and value-driven solutions that consistently meet the evolving needs of businesses and institutions across sectors.


Led by a team of seasoned professionals, the firm leverages its extensive resources to offer a comprehensive suite of services, ensuring clients receive expert guidance across every facet of their financial and regulatory needs.


Once engaged, our foremost priority is to gain a thorough understanding of the subject matter at hand. At Bansal & Agrawal, every client is valued, and we are committed to delivering personalized attention and strategic insight. Our unwavering commitment to independence and integrity ensures that we provide objective, high-quality advice—enabling us to serve our clients' needs in the most effective and efficient manner.

Our Leader

ANURAG BANSAL

Anurag Bansal, Founder of Bansal & Agrawal, is a member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI) since 1998. With over two and a half decades of professional experience, he has been instrumental in advising a diverse clientele on securing government subsidies, financial assistance, and policy concessions under various Central and State Government schemes

Throughout his career, Mr. Bansal has led teams in executing a wide range of high-impact assignments, including Statutory Audits, Concurrent Audits, Internal Audits, and Due Diligence exercises for Government Departments and private sector entities alike.

His expertise extends beyond domestic boundaries—he has successfully supported clients in making cross-border investments and setting up industrial ventures internationally. Backed by more than 25 years of experience in Tax Advisory and Assurance Services, Mr. Bansal brings a rare blend of strategic insight, technical proficiency, and practical business acumen to the firm's leadership.
